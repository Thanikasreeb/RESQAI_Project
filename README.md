## ResQ AI – Smart AI Agents for Child Rescue Reporting

# Title of the Project
## ResQ AI – Smart AI Agents for Child Rescue Reporting

An AI-powered, voice-enabled child safety reporting system designed to help citizens easily report vulnerable or at-risk children and enable welfare authorities to respond quickly and effectively.

# About
Street children and minors in vulnerable situations often go unnoticed due to the absence of fast, reliable, and structured reporting mechanisms. Existing systems mainly rely on manual helpline calls, which are slow, inconsistent, and inaccessible to people with limited literacy or time constraints.

ResQ AI addresses this challenge by introducing an intelligent, voice-first AI reporting platform. Citizens can report at-risk children simply by speaking or chatting with an AI agent. The system collects essential information such as location, child description, visible risks, and urgency level through natural conversation.

The platform verifies reports using trust-scoring mechanisms, securely stores validated data in the cloud, and automatically routes cases to the nearest NGOs or child welfare authorities through a centralized dashboard. ResQ AI focuses on accessibility, privacy, scalability, and rapid response to strengthen child protection efforts.

# Features

1.AI Voice-Based Reporting

> Voice and chat interaction for easy reporting without forms or typing
> Supports users with limited literacy or technical knowledge

2.Conversational AI Agent

> Natural language interaction with dynamic follow-up questions
> Extracts structured data from unstructured speech

3.Verification and Trust Scoring

> Detects inconsistencies, missing data, and suspicious behavior
> Reduces false or misleading reports

4.Cloud-Based Secure Storage

> Encrypted and scalable storage of validated reports
> Real-time data synchronization

5.Automated Geo-Based Routing

> Routes cases to the nearest and most appropriate NGO or authority
> Prioritizes high-risk and urgent cases

6.Responder Dashboard

> Centralized case monitoring and status updates
> Assignment of field officers and intervention tracking

7.Privacy and Security

> Secure APIs, encrypted communication, and access control
> Confidential handling of sensitive child-related data

## System Architecture
The ResQ AI system follows a modular, multi-layered architecture:

1.AI Voice Interaction Layer

> Speech-to-Text (STT) and NLP-based conversational engine
> Collects and structures report details

2.Verification and Trust Scoring Layer

> Analyzes consistency, urgency, and credibility
> Filters false or low-quality reports

3.Cloud Processing and Storage Layer

> Secure cloud storage of validated case files
> Real-time synchronization and scalability

4.Automated Routing Layer

> Maps cases to nearest NGOs or welfare authorities
> Sends structured case data via secure APIs

5.Responder Dashboard Layer

> Case tracking, status updates, and intervention logs
> Centralized monitoring for authorities

6.Security and Privacy Layer

> Data encryption, access control, and compliance enforcement
